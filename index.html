About

I'm Eng. Denis Mutuma, a Mechanical Engineering Specialist and Technologist with hands-on expertise spanning precision machining, structural fabrication, and advanced mechanical systems design.

My work bridges classical mechanical engineering principles — thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, kinematics — with practical fabrication skills in welding, grinding, and CNC-style machining operations.

I'm currently developing cutting-edge projects in thermoelectric power generation and exploring the frontier of non-electric magnetic drive systems, pushing the boundaries of what mechanical engineering can achieve.

⚙️ Approach

Engineering solutions rooted in first-principles thinking. Every design begins with deep analysis of forces, flows, and material behaviour before a single weld or cut is made.

🔬 Focus Areas

Energy conversion systems, mechanical power transmission, precision fabrication, and sustainable machine design without reliance on conventional electrical infrastructure.

Projects

🧪 Prototyped · Under Testing

Thermoelectric Power Generation System

A working prototype that converts heat energy directly into electrical power using thermoelectric modules. The system exploits temperature gradients across thermoelectric elements (Seebeck effect) to generate usable electricity — a practical, low-maintenance solution for off-grid or waste-heat recovery applications. Currently in active testing and performance optimisation phase.

Thermodynamics Energy Conversion Seebeck Effect Heat Transfer Prototype

Magnetic Drive Machine (Non-Electric)

An innovative mechanical system that harnesses permanent magnetic forces to drive motion without conventional electrical input. Explores magnetic flux interaction, rotor-stator geometries, and torque optimisation in a novel non-electric drive configuration.
